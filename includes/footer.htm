About G.A.N.G

God's Appointed New Generation is an online godly group or ministry based on youth setting that mirrors some of the church activities especially from the Pentecostal church.

We offer all godly services like bible teaching, training ministers, interpreting scriptures, dream interpretation and answering all kinds of godly questions.

Our Services Include:
  • Sermon recordings and teachings
  • Bible reading with multiple translations
  • Spiritual music and worship
  • Community discussions and Q&A
  • Dream interpretation and spiritual guidance
  • Minister training programs
  • File uploads and media sharing
  • Platform rating and feedback
Our Goal
To train self-reliant and Holy Spirit guided ministers to stand vividly for the truth in the end time period.
Our Vision
Creating a physically and spiritually complete church in the end times.
Our Mission
Equipping youths with the necessary knowledge, understanding, and wisdom fostering both spiritual and physical competence.
